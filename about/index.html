CS 2261 @ Georgia Tech

Welcome to GBA GT.

CS 2261: Media Device Architecture is a Georgia Tech course designed to teach fundamental CS concepts in C, using the GameBoy Advance as the target platform.

This site is owned and operated by former lecturers, teaching assistants, and students of the course.

It has no official connection to the Georgia Institute of Technology.
